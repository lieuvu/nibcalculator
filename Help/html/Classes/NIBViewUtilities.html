+ toggleHiddenButtonsOfRow:basedOnReference:

Toggle hidden buttons of a row based on a reference dictionary. It will show the first group buttons and hide the second group buttons if they are hidden and shown respectively. Otherwise, it will hide the first group buttons and show the second group buttons if they are shown and hidden respectively.

Discussion
